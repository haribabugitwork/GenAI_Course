Course Description

Generative AI is revolutionizing the way machines generate text, images, and other creative content, with applications in chatbots, image synthesis, and AI-driven automation. This course provides a deep dive into the architectures that power Generative AI, covering Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs), Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs), Long Short-Term Memory (LSTMs), Attention Mechanisms, and Transformers. Students will learn the foundational principles behind these models and how they evolve from traditional neural networks to state-of-the-art Transformer architectures like BERT, GPT, and CLIP.

Through hands-on assignments and a final project, students will implement, fine-tune, and deploy generative models for tasks such as text generation, sequence modeling, and multimodal AI applications, gaining a strong practical understanding of deep learning techniques and their real-world impact.

Prerequisites

  • Proficiency in Python: You should be comfortable in Python packages like numpy, pandas. All class assignments will be in Python.
  • College Calculus, Linear Algebra: You should be comfortable taking derivatives and understanding matrix vector operations and notation.
  • Basic Probability and Statistics: You should know basics of probabilities, Gaussian distributions, mean, standard deviation, etc.
